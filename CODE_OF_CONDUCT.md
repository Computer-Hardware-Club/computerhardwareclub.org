# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This repository supports a student club community. Contributors are expected to be respectful, constructive, and practical in reviews, discussions, and contributions.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in technical and non-technical discussion
- Assume good intent, but communicate clearly when something is incorrect
- Give actionable review feedback
- Keep club spaces welcoming to beginners and experienced members alike

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Dismissing contributors for lack of experience
- Spam, trolling, or intentionally disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Club maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions and discussions that violate these expectations. Serious concerns can be reported to `computerhardware.clubs@oregonstate.edu`.
